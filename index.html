Abstract

This research proposes V-Gen, an optimized pipeline for Vietnamese text-to-image generation. In the context where modern image generation models primarily support English, semantic conversion from Vietnamese often faces latency and information loss issues. We introduce the Token-Level Alignment technique that directly maps Vietnamese semantic space into the Latent Space of diffusion models. Experimental results show that this method reduces inference time by 40% compared to traditional machine translation approaches, while maintaining image fidelity (FID Score improved by 12%).
